"What I have learned is that many things are basically the same in different disciplines. My Philosophy

I believe that the boundaries between disciplines are often artificial. The most innovative solutions come from recognizing patterns across different domains and applying approaches from one field to solve problems in another.

My background in mechatronics engineering provides me with a unique foundation to explore these connections, as it already sits at the intersection of mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering.

Through continuous experimentation and a willingness to try new approaches, I've found that seemingly impossible challenges often have solutions waiting to be discovered.
